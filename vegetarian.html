Ants on a Log???

Growing up this was how my mom got me to eat a healthy snack. My mom knew I struggled eating healthy snacks so as I ate the snack she would tell me stories about how the "ants" got stuck in the peanut butter or about how the "ants" were going on an adventure in a boat.

What you will need is celery, peanut butter, and raisins.

All you need to do is cut and prepare the celery then put peanut butter through the middle of the groove of the celery, until you have the amount you like, then sprinkle the raisins on top of the peanut butter
